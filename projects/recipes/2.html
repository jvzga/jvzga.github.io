Recipe 2:Tortillas

The most basic, yet most common item in any hispanic dish, they are easy to make and can be adapted into any dish like tacos, burritos, quesadillas, etc. All it takes is some imagination to make anything you want with them.

Every meal needs a tortilla, its not complete without it, the basic is what we need

- My Mom

Prep Time Approx:15 Minutes

Serving Size:Depends on how many dough balls were made


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of corn flour
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons
    or pinches of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water

Instructions:

  1. Mix the corn flour and water in a bowl along with the salt
  2. Knead the mixture until you get a soft dough,
    TIP:if its dry keep adding 1 tablespoon of water until the dough is consistent,
    if its wet, do the same thing with 1 tablespoon of dough
  3. Make small and equal amounts of balls from the dough
  4. Flatten the balls with a tortilla press
    with plastic or a flatten them with your hands
  5. Cook the tortillas on medium heat
    with a skillet/pan and flip them every 30 seconds
  6. Repeat the process 3 to 4 times until
    spots of brown and black appear and check that its stable
  7. Wrap the tortillas in a cloth
    to keep them warm while your cooking the rest
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 until you run out of the dough balls

Enjoy your homemade tortillas! Serving size will depend on how many balls of dough you make.
