Tortillas

The most basic, yet most common item in any hispanic dish, they are easy to make and can be adapted into any dish like tacos, burritos, quesadillas, etc.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of corn flour
  • 1 or 2 teaspoon/pinches of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water

Instructions:

  1. Mix the corn flour and water in a bowl along with the salt
  2. Knead the mixture until you get a soft dough, if its dry added a tablespoon of water
  3. Make small and equal amounts of balls from the dough
  4. Flatten the balls with a tortilla press with plastic or a flatten them with your hands
  5. Cook the tortillas on medium heat with a skillet/pan and flip them every 30 seconds
  6. Repeat the process until it spots of brown and black appear and check that its stable
  7. Wrap the tortillas in a cloth to keep them warm while your cooking the rest
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 until you run out of the dough balls

Enjoy your homemade tortillas!
